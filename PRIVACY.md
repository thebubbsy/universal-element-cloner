# Privacy Policy for Universal Element Cloner

**Last Updated**: February 8, 2026

## Overview
Universal Element Cloner is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection
**We collect NO data.** Period.

The extension operates entirely locally in your browser and does not:
- Collect personal information
- Track your browsing history
- Send data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Store data on remote servers

## Local Storage
The extension uses Chrome's local storage API only to save:
- Your theme preference (dark/light mode)
- Scroll speed setting
- Capture count (for display purposes only)

This data never leaves your device and is only used to enhance your experience with the extension.

## Permissions Explained

### activeTab
Allows the extension to access the currently active tab when you explicitly use the extension. This is required to capture DOM elements from the page you're viewing.

### storage
Enables saving your preferences (theme, settings) locally in your browser.

### downloads
Required to export captured elements as HTML files to your computer.

### scripting
Allows injection of the content script for element selection functionality.

### sidePanel
Provides the extension's user interface in a side panel.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

## Data Security
Since we don't collect any data, there's no data to secure, lose, or breach. All operations happen locally in your browser.

## Children's Privacy
This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or any age).

## Changes to Privacy Policy
Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in the extension's update notes and on our GitHub repository.

You have complete control over the extension. You can:
